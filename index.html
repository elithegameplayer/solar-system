Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun (A.K.A sol) but it is NOT the hottest because it does not have atmosphere so the heat does not get trapped. The surface just absorbs the heat and the sun's rays are always blazing down on it making it hotter. Mercury's top heat is 801. Mercury has no moons.

Venus

Venus is the 2nd closest planet to the sun. It is also the hottest planet. Venus is the hottest planet because it has an atmosphere to trap the heat. Venus also always has erupting volcanoes and flowing lava lakes on the surface. Venus's max heat is 880 degrees. Venus has no moons.
